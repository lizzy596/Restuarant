About Us

Angelo's was establised in established in 1983 by Timothy and Anne Rossi and quickly became a Lakewood neighborhood establishment.

The dishes offered are a delightful blend of authentic Italian and Italian-American dishes. Favorites include authentic Neopolitan Pizza, fired in our classic wood-fired oven at temperatures of 900 degrees and cooking in less than two minutes for the delicate, light and leopard charred dough famous throughout Naples. Other favorites include our delicious Chicken Parmesan and House Spaghetti, made with our signature tomato sauce.

The atmosphere is always warm and inviting, with hand-drawn murals setting the scene and ensuring you'll always have a meal to remember.
